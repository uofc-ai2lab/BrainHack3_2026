About the Hackathon

The AI Neuroimaging Hackathon will take place during the afternoon of the first day of the 2nd ISMRM Brazilian Chapter Annual Meeting 2026 . The event will introduce participants to artificial intelligence applications in medical imaging, with a particular focus on Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI).

Highlights

  • Hands-on, follow-along exercises
  • Focus on AI in MRI and neuroimaging
  • Real clinical imaging data: DTI + T1
  • No prior setup is required — participants only need to bring their own laptop

Who Can Join?

  • Researchers, students, and clinicians
  • Participants interested in AI in healthcare, neuroimaging, and medical data science
  • No prerequisites required — all levels are welcome

Participants Will

  • Follow a guided workflow using DTI and T1 neuroimaging data
  • Learn practical approaches for data analysis and segmentation
  • Explore real-world AI applications in neuroimaging

Final Phase

  • Final project presentations
  • Awards and prizes for outstanding contributions

Registration

  • Limited spots available due to venue capacity
  • Attendees interested in a broader experience are invited to participate in the full ISMRM Brazilian Chapter Annual Meeting

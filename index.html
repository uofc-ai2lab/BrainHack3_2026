About the Hackathon

The AI Neuroimaging Hackathon will take place during the afternoon of the first day of the 2nd ISMRM Brazilian Chapter Annual Meeting 2026. The event will introduce participants to artificial intelligence applications in medical imaging, with a particular focus on Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI).

Participants Will

  • Follow a guided, hands-on workflow using DTI and T1 neuroimaging data
  • Learn practical approaches for data analysis and segmentation
  • Engage with real-world AI applications in neuroimaging
  • Present their work during the final project presentation session

Highlights

  • Hands-on, follow-along exercises
  • Focus on AI in MRI and neuroimaging
  • Real clinical imaging data (DTI + T1)
  • No prior setup required
  • No computational resources required
  • Just bring your laptop — no installation required

Final Phase

The hackathon will conclude with final project presentations and awards recognizing outstanding contributions.

  • Final project presentations
  • Awards and prizes for outstanding contributions

Additional details regarding judging criteria and prizes will be announced soon.

Who Can Join?

Researchers, students, and clinicians interested in AI in healthcare, neuroimaging, and medical data science are encouraged to participate.

No prerequisites are required. Participants of all backgrounds and experience levels are welcome.
